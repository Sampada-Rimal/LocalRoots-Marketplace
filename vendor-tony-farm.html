Tony's Family Farm

Three generations growing leafy greens and hearty winter veg with sustainable practices.

Tony learned from his parents how to work with the weather, not against it. The farm still irrigates with recycled dam water and hand-harvests most crops.

Small harvests go out daily to keep items crisp and reduce waste. The team prioritizes soil health, companion planting, and regional seed varieties that thrive locally.

Customer favorites include butter lettuce, cavolo nero, and their pesto starter kits.
